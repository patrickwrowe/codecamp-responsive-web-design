INTRO

Newfoundland and Labrador is home to some of the most beautiful and challenging climbing locations in the world. With a variety of locations and difficulties, there is something for everyone. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced climber, Newfoundland and Labrador has something for you.

LOCATIONS

Gros Morne National Park

Gros Morne National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located on the west coast of Newfoundland. The park is home to some of the most beautiful and challenging climbing locations in the world. With a variety of locations and difficulties, there is something for everyone. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced climber, Gros Morne National Park has something for you.
